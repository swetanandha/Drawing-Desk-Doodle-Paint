# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

[Your Company Name] ("we", "our", or "us") operates the `Drawing Desk - Doodle & Paint` mobile application (the "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the App.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you create in the App

The App allows you to create drawings and artwork.

Drawings you create are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

We do not currently require you to create an account to use the App.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

Depending on how you use the App and which system or third-party services are active, certain information may be collected automatically, such as:

- device information
- app version and operating system version
- crash or diagnostic information
- purchase and subscription status information provided by Apple
- advertising-related data used to serve and measure ads

### 2.3 In-app purchases and subscriptions

Subscriptions and related purchase processing are handled by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit.

We do not receive your full payment card details.

Apple may provide us with limited subscription or transaction status information necessary to determine whether premium access should be unlocked in the App.

### 2.4 Rewarded ads

The App may offer rewarded ads that temporarily unlock certain features, including `Dash` and `Neon`.

When ads are shown, third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, which can include:

- device identifiers
- IP address
- approximate location
- advertising interaction data
- performance and diagnostics data

This data is processed according to the provider's own privacy policies and platform requirements.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- operate and maintain the App
- save and restore your drawings on your device
- unlock subscription features
- provide rewarded ad access where applicable
- improve performance, reliability, and user experience
- comply with legal obligations

## 4. How Your Drawings Are Handled

Your drawings are primarily stored locally on your device.

If you choose to export or share a drawing, the content is shared only through the action you initiate.

We do not currently provide a user account system or cloud gallery inside the App.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services, including:

- Apple App Store and StoreKit for subscriptions and in-app purchases
- Google AdMob for rewarded ads
- Google User Messaging Platform for consent and privacy messaging

These third parties may collect, use, and process information under their own privacy terms.

You should review:

- Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 6. Legal Bases and Regional Rights

If you are located in a region with privacy laws that grant you specific rights, such as the EEA, UK, or certain US states, you may have rights including:

- access to your personal data
- correction of inaccurate data
- deletion of certain data
- objection to certain processing
- withdrawal of consent where processing is based on consent

Because much of the App's content is stored locally on your device, some requests may require you to manage or delete data directly from your device.

You may contact us using the information below for privacy-related requests.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Locally stored drawings remain on your device until you delete the App, clear the data, or remove the drawings yourself.

Third-party providers may retain data under their own retention policies.
